Demographic Hypothesis · v1.0
Listener live · daily scan against public signal

Preliminary market segmentation & persona research for DUST — twenty hypothesized segments, a scoring matrix, and a daily listener that tests each hypothesis against live public signal. The listener is the deliverable; it's designed to inform brand and messaging strategy, and to feed every downstream growth process — cohort selection, paid creative, partnership targeting, CRM segmentation. Every claim is a falsifiable bet, revised as the data comes in. Context lives on the TAM, Targets & Risks, and Methodology pages.
