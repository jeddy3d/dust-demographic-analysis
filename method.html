Section 2 Mechanism

The validation loop.
How this document earns — and keeps — its accuracy.

The DUST segmentation listener is a read-only signal system. It reads public subreddit conversation from each hypothesized segment, never posts, DMs, or votes, and converts raw language into aggregate signal this research can be revised against. The loop below describes what it does and the privacy policy it enforces. Downstream, its output feeds cohort selection, messaging research, creative targeting, and partnership prioritization — it's infrastructure, not a one-off study.

  1. 01
    Listen
    Pull new posts from each segment's native subreddits on a daily cadence, via Reddit's public JSON endpoints. Unauthenticated, rate-limited under 50 req/min, and a descriptive User-Agent identifying DUST.
  2. 02
    Match
    Each post is matched against the segment's positive pattern library (e.g. "lucid dream," "HRV," "vivid pregnancy dreams," "night sweats"). Misses are surfaced so the library can grow with how the community actually talks.
  3. 03
    Score
    A Claude-based scorer rates each candidate author on language fit and stated intent, applying per-segment psychographic signals. Author handles never leave the pipeline.
  4. 04
    Aggregate
    Only privacy-safe aggregates are persisted: 7-day post volume, unique author counts, top phrases, top subreddits. Policy gates what is exposed per segment (see below).
  5. 05
    Revise
    Monthly diffs against this hypothesis: segments promoted, demoted, or retired; pattern libraries updated; new segments proposed when signal warrants. Every revision is versioned.
Section 3 Privacy

Privacy policy & outreach tiering.
What the listener is allowed to expose — per segment, without exception.

Policy Applies to Exposed Never exposed
candidate Lucid dreamers · Biohackers · Creatives · Consciousness · Sleep-curious Post volume · unique author counts · top phrases Author handles · post bodies · DMs (none sent, ever)
messaging_only Pregnant & new mothers · Perimenopausal women Post volume · top phrases Author counts · author handles · post bodies
earned_only Clinical (PTSD / grief / nightmare) Post volume only Phrases · author counts · author handles · post bodies
The listener never posts, comments, votes, follows, or messages. It is a read-only observer. Clinical segments remain earned-only in both hypothesis and validation — signal informs strategy, never outreach. Two independent privacy passes (rollup.py and export_public_pulse.py) filter phrases through a stopword + regex gate and drop anything shared by fewer than 5 distinct authors.
Status · listener live. The cohort pipeline runs daily at 08:00 UTC via GitHub Actions. Each run fetches new posts, scores them against current segment definitions, writes the SQLite database back into its private repo, and pushes a privacy-filtered pulse.json to this site. Full rationale — including author handles, post bodies, and Claude rationales — never leaves the private cohort repo.
